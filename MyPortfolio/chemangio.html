Che Mangio

App

CheMangio is an iOS/Android application developed by medical specialists that informs you what you can eat for the gastrointestinal disorders and disease you suffer from.The proposed contents are personalized. The user can characterize his profile by entering the dietary preferences (vegan, vegetarian, omnivore, gluten-free, lactose-free) and the diseases he suffers from in order to consult Foods, Beverages and Recipes filtered according to the choices. cheMangio provides in addition to a diet based on your dietary preferences and your gastrointestinal problems, a database of thousands of foods and beverages, hundreds of recipes recommended for the user, a list of medical specialists and nutritionists closest to the user, scientific news and food curiosities constantly updated by our scientific team.
